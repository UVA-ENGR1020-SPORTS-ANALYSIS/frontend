# Game Progression Rules

This document outlines the standard flow and rules for the different game modes in the application.

## 1. Single Team Mode
In this mode, a single team plays without any opponent. The objective is to track player shooting performance.

**Workflow:**
1. **Shooting Round:** Each player on the team takes exactly **5 shots**.
2. **Results:** After all players have finished their shots, the game directly transitions to the **Stats Page** to display individual and team metrics.
*(Note: There is no Phase 2 or banning mechanics in Single Team Mode).*

---

## 2. Multi-Team Mode (PvP)
In this mode, multiple teams compete against each other. If there are 4 teams, the game follows a **Tournament Bracket** format.

**Workflow:**
1. **Round 1 (Initial Shooting):** 
   - Both competing teams take their shots simultaneously.
   - Each player takes exactly **5 shots**.
2. **First Stats Reveal:** 
   - After Round 1 concludes, players are taken to the **Stats Page** to view the performance metrics for all players.
3. **Ban Zone Phase:** 
   - Based on the opponent's stats from Round 1, each team selects **one Hot Zone** of their opponent to "Ban". 
   - A banned zone implies the opponent may not shoot from that location.
4. **Round 2 (Restricted Shooting):**
   - Both teams shoot another round.
   - Players must respect the banned zone imposed by their opponent.
5. **Final Stats & Results:**
   - After Round 2 concludes, the game transitions to the final **Stats Page**, showing the overall performance, the impact of the banned zones, and the final outcome of the matchup.
